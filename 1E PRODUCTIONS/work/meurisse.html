MEURISSE

CREATING FUTURE HERITAGE. In 1845, Meurisse started as the original Belgian chocolatier. In 2019, the great-great-great grandsons of Adolphe Meurisse came to us to continue the legacy.  Six generations down the line, we relaunched the iconic chocolate with a determination to keep making remarkable chocolate whilst simultaneously creating a positive social and environmental impact.

“We wanted to capture the spirit of escapism. With each piece of chocolate, there is this moment of transportation to another place, even if only fleeting. Each bar acts as a portal, allowing ourselves that momentary opportunity to escape and travel the world, through unique and reimagined flavours.””

Radek Wojcik, Creative Director.
