THE IMPACT OF CREATIVITY WITH MIAMI DESIGN DISTRICT

PLAY
TEASER FILM

How do you translate a vision into identity and then identity into a brand capable of uniting a community?

Both a creative neighbourhood and luxury shopping destination, Miami Design District is a placemaking icon. Dedicated to innovative fashion, art, design and dining, it is an ever-evolving cultural hub. When creating the brand identity, it was clear to Made Thought that creative bravery was not just optional.

As we reflect on the creative partnership–almost twenty years in the making–our founding partner Paul Austin sits down with MDD's founder Craig Robins. They discuss what it means to capture a sense of place and how to evoke lasting impact in today’s creative sphere.
