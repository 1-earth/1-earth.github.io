MIDWEIGHT STRATEGIST

WE'RE LOOKING FOR

PROGRESSIVE THINKING AND CULTURAL CONNECTIVITY

As part of our core mission of ‘being famous for progressive thinking and beautiful design to transform and elevate’, the Midweight Strategist is fundamental in helping to shape a progressive vision for our clients that will direct and lead to outstanding creative work that we will deliver.

A confident, personable and culturally informed style is needed that can flex to varying brand sectors and ultimately engage clients with our heartfelt, inspiring and engaging approach to work.



RESPONSIBILITIES

— Be an integral support for the Senior Strategy team throughout the Made Thought strategy process.

— Develop strong trend forecasting and analysis skills within a variety of sectors.

— Utilise design knowledge to create clear briefs for the Studio team and build on communication between Strategy and Design teams.

— Incorporate cultural awareness and experience when developing strategy in new sectors.

— Compile and create strategy decks for internal and external presentations.

— Maintain a good understanding of client requirements and messaging and be confident with supporting and developing a strategic direction.


SKILLS & EXPERIENCE

— Strategy background within a Creative Studio or Advertising Agency

— Excellent writing skills, able to adapt to Made Thought tone of voice

— Culturally diverse and aware

— Accuracy and quality shown in all work presented

— Confident, enthusiastic and personable style

— Communication skills will be key, adapting to varying client personalities and internal teams

— Excellent organisational skills and adept in multi-tasking

— Have a familiarity with data, research and trends, and processing provided reports from clients, whilst balancing analytics with a desire to think and search outside of the box.


EXPERTISE

— Keynote

— Adobe Creative Suite (not essential but beneficial)

— Creative writing

— Building presentation documents

— Excellent presentation skills

— Demonstrable interest in culture and creativity
