1EARTH IS:

A platform for empowering creatives making positive changes within their communities.

We are an open community bound by our shared core values of equality, inclusivity, and accessibility.

We believe that as creatives, it is our duty to lead and inspire collective action, organising efforts for broader impact to shape the future we envision.
