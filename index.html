This is a map of all initiatives making a positive impact within their communities.

The nodes represent each initiative & contains information on their activities.

Use the menus in the top lefthand bar to filter for relevant information.

For example, click the box 'NETWORK' and select 'COMMUNITY SUPPORT DIRECTORY' to a specified network that relates to all the ways you can volunteer & help out in your local community

You can then also use other filters - use the “Categories” box and select 'Design' to see all communities that are related to design.
